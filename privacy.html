PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

TL;DR: Outpace collects the minimum data needed to run step leaderboards among your friends. We don't sell your data, we don't run ads, and we don't use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Your raw phone number and contact data never leave your device.

1. WHO WE ARE

Outpace is operated by Virtual Pirates LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). Outpace is an iMessage extension and companion iOS app that creates daily step-count leaderboards among friends.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

HealthKit Step Data

We read your daily step count from Apple Health (HealthKit). This data is synced to our backend once per day to generate leaderboards. We only access step count data -- no other health metrics.

Phone Number

You provide your phone number during registration. Your raw phone number is stored locally on your device only. Before being sent to our server, your phone number is hashed using SHA-256 with a cryptographic salt. The server only ever receives and stores the hash, not your actual phone number.

Contacts (Phone Numbers Only)

With your permission, we access the phone numbers in your device contacts to find friends who also use Outpace. All phone numbers are SHA-256 hashed on your device before being sent to the server. Raw contact names, emails, addresses, and other contact data never leave your device.

Name

You provide a first name or nickname during onboarding. This name is displayed on leaderboards so your friends can identify you.

Device Token

We collect your Apple Push Notification (APNs) device token to send you daily leaderboard notifications (e.g., a 9 PM reminder to check standings). You can disable notifications at any time through iOS Settings.

3. WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • No third-party analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude)
  • No advertising SDKs or ad tracking
  • No location data
  • No browsing history or device fingerprinting
  • No sale or sharing of personal data with third parties

4. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • Step data -- to generate and display daily leaderboards among your friends
  • Hashed phone number -- to identify your account and match you with friends
  • Hashed contacts -- to find which of your contacts also use Outpace
  • Name -- to display your identity on leaderboards
  • Device token -- to deliver push notifications about daily leaderboards

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Your data is stored on Supabase, a hosted PostgreSQL platform running on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure in the United States. All API communication uses HTTPS encryption in transit. Database access is protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your account data and step history for as long as your account is active. Daily leaderboard data is generated and stored indefinitely to provide historical rankings. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. DATA SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your step counts are visible only to friends who are also Outpace users (determined by mutual contact matching). Leaderboard data is shared only within the context of iMessage conversations where you choose to send leaderboard cards.

8. APPLE HEALTHKIT DISCLOSURE

Outpace integrates with Apple HealthKit to read step count data. In compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:

  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising or marketing purposes
  • HealthKit data is not sold to data brokers or third parties
  • HealthKit data is used solely for the app's primary purpose of generating step leaderboards
  • HealthKit data is not stored in iCloud or any unsecured storage

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Outpace is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

10. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Revoke HealthKit or Contacts permissions at any time via iOS Settings
  • Disable push notifications at any time via iOS Settings

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via push notification. Your continued use of Outpace after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
